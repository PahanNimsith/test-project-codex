FAQ

Questions answered

How long does it take to build my website?
Most sites are live within 3–5 days. A landing page can go live in 48 hours. Larger 8-page sites take about 7 days. I'll give you a specific timeline in our first call.
What's included in the price?
Design, development, content writing, image sourcing, animations, contact forms, mobile optimisation, and basic SEO setup. Everything to make your site look and work professionally from day one.
Do you handle domain and hosting?
Yes. I manage everything — buying your domain, deploying to our secure cloud infrastructure, and connecting it all. You don't need to touch a single technical setting.
Can I update the website myself after launch?
You can — but most clients prefer to just WhatsApp me changes since I turn them around the same day. On monthly plans, unlimited updates are included so you never need to touch code.
How do payments work?
50% upfront to start, 50% on launch. For monthly plans, billed at the start of each month. Sri Lankan bank transfer, or international payment via PayPal/Wise.
Do you work with international clients?
Absolutely. I work with clients across the UK, Australia, Singapore, UAE, and more. Everything is handled over WhatsApp, email, and video call — location is no barrier.
What if I want changes after launch?
Revisions during the build are unlimited. After launch, small changes (text, photos) are free for 7 days. After that, join a monthly plan or pay per-change — no hidden fees, always transparent.
